# NexSpy Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

NexSpy is a local pass-and-play party game. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

## Information We Collect

NexSpy does not collect personal information.

The app does not require an account, login, email address, phone number, name, contacts, location, photos, microphone access, camera access, or payment information.

## Analytics, Ads, and Tracking

NexSpy does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

NexSpy does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies.

## Game Data

Game settings, role assignment, selected words, and timer state are used only inside the app during gameplay.

This gameplay information stays on the device while the game is running and is not sent to us or to a third party.

## Data Sharing

Because NexSpy does not collect personal information, we do not sell, rent, trade, or share personal information with third parties.

## Children

NexSpy does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Data Retention and Deletion

NexSpy does not store personal information on our servers.

To remove local app data from a device, delete the app from the device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy if the app's features or data practices change. Any updates will be posted in this file.

## Contact

For privacy questions, support requests, or bug reports, please open an issue in the GitHub repository where this app is published.
